The Best Kdramas According to Nandini

My top 3 K-dramas of all-time

Goblin

Goblin is tale of immortal person looking for love. It has very cute guys and they have good chemistry

The Legend of the Blue Sea

This is love story between mermaid and a man. They face the same fate again with same characters from Josen and tries to change their fate.

Whats wrong with Secretary Kim

This drama is about narcisst ceo who loves no one but himself and then tries to retain his secretary who wants to leave.
